Overview

If you ever look into a high-quality faux leather jacket, a wallet, or a handbag, know that we may probably be involved in that. ARk is the largest PVC clothing, vinyl fabric, and PVC jacket material supplier for PVC apparel in India and abroad. Stepping into the fashion sector, we have now become one of the largest synthetic leather exporters in India. Our artificial leather is shipped abroad and widely used in the fashion industry.

Our Mania PVC Leather for fashion industry is of high quality that meets the international standard. It is highly durable and weather-resistant. Our PVC leather clothes for jackets garments closely resemble real leather and no wonder they are the preferred choice of our customers. The PVC leather for fashion is scratch-resistant, fire-resistant, and water-repellent. It is smooth enough but is able to take the elaborate sewing without any hassle on the synthetic leather. We, at ARK, adhere to the industry and environment norms during the manufacturing processes and are compliant with all the rules and regulations. We have been ranked among the top synthetic leather exporters because we customize the PVC leather for the fashion industry according to the client needs.
