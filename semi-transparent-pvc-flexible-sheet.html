Overview

ARKOM has been proudly associated as the top translucent PVC vinyl film sheet manufacturer in Gujarat, India. We are constantly trying to innovate and pass on the benefits to our customers at affordable prices and at the same time offer excellent quality products.

ARKOM manufactures translucent PVC flexible sheets that have a wide range of applications for commercial, residential, and healthcare purposes. In addition to transparent, opaque PVC sheets, ARKOM also manufactures translucent flexible PVC sheets that have a host of advantages over others.

The translucent PVC flexible sheets are anti-bacterial, fire-resistant, water-resistant, UV-resistant, and anti-fungal. We, the finest PVC manufacturer in Gujarat, India are into the translucent PVC flexible sheets production and our processes are fully compliant with the industrial and environmental norms and standards. We manufacture translucent PVC flexible sheets with clear lamination so that the color stays and does not fade away easily. We manufacture translucent PVC flexible sheets of varying thickness, designs, and textures as per the custom requirements.
