Inspection Process

Inspection of Incoming Material

Also called incoming inspection. It consists of checking and verifying all raw materials (chemicals, resins, plasticizers, pigments, substances, etc.) purchased. Our quality team thoroughly checks all incoming raw materials and ensures they are in order for further processing. If the raw material does not meet our standards, it will be rejected and sent back to the supplier.

We have a fully equipped laboratory for testing and checking the incoming material, consisting of devices such as:

Brookfield Viscometers Hydrometers Grind meters Karl Fischer Titrometers Coater-Laboratory Bar coater Ovens Fabric yarn counters Tensile tester

Online Inspection

The online inspection consists of reviewing and approving the first part according to customer specifications and our internal specifications during production. It is an important step in ensuring that the raw material is adequate according to internal and external specifications. Colour, feel, weight, thickness, finish and all other mandatory requirements are verified before proceeding with further production.

Some of the devices this laboratory uses for the control process include:

Tensile Tester Vessel Gauge Spectrophotometer Bend Tester Abrasion Tester Thickness Gauge Scales
