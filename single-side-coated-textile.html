Overview

ARK manufactures single side coated textiles that are used widely for awnings, sunshade, tents, canopy, and truck covers. We are positioned as one of the best PVC awning, single coated vinyl awning fabric manufacturers in India because we deliver the best quality single side coated textiles for all the industry spanning from residences, commercial complexes to the transport industry. The awning is the most commonly used example for single side coated textiles and we manufacture the fixed awning, retractable awning or the vertical awning textiles in India.

The single side coated textiles at ARK can be tailored to application-specific requirements quite readily and at low cost. These enable significant cost savings when compared to other materials. The substrates of core materials can range from silk, cotton, polyester, carbon fabric, to polyaramid and metal. The PVC (Poly Vinyl Chloride) is the most commonly used polymer for the coating. While they are widely used. The PVC coated textiles are resistant to water, chemicals, oil, and grease and make the material even more sturdy and flexible. It is up to the customer to select the type of core material and the coating polymer depending on the use and cost.

At ARK, we make single side coated textiles that are in tune with the needs of the customer. We are ranked the best among the top PVC manufacturers in India, and we work hard to achieve the best in quality and customer satisfaction.
