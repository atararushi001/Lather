Overview

When we think of travel luggage, we need that the suitcases and bags are of high-quality that is sturdy and last long. Our ARKOM Spendid PVC leather for travel luggage industry is all of that and much more. We manufacture PVC leather for bags, suitcases, handbags, and purses.

Our ARKOM Spendid PVC leather series is so designed so that it is capable to carry heavy loads. They are scratch-resistant, anti-bacterial, fire-resistant, water-resistant, and odorless. The ARKOM Spendid range comes in varied hues and texture from the gloss to the matte finish. It is lead and heavy metal-free. It is seam-resistant and can take on sewing easily. We, at ark-splendid Flex, are committed to maintaining high standards and adhere to all the compliance rules and environmental norms.

We are the finest leather travel bags, PVC leather material for bags manufacturer in India. We see to it that our client gets what exactly is needed by him. We fully customize our manufacturing processes based on customer requirements.
